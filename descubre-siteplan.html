Descubre - Site Plan

Site Name

Descubre - This name represents a clothing brand that highlights the culture and beauty of Mexico through urban wear.

Optional domain availability: descubre-mexico.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for showcasing and selling urban wear inspired by Mexican culture, offering product information, customer reviews, and a shopping cart feature.

Scenarios

  • How can I best express my Mexican culture while looking stylish?
  • What color options are available in each shirt?
  • What are the bestselling items?
  • Does this brand only offer clothing, or do they offer other branded items?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • #151F12 (Verde Nocturno) - Used for headings and accents.
  • #273E22 (Nopal) - Used for text and background elements.
  • #F4B19D (Sunset Guava) - Used for highlights and buttons.
  • #DB3D02 (Chiltepin) - Used for warnings and special notices.
  • #F4E0C7 (Duna de Arena) - Used for background.
