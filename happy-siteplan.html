Happiness Corner - Site Plan

Site Name

Happiness Corner - This name represents a site focused on teaching the importance, myths, and steps to achieve happiness.

Optional domain availability: happiness-corner.com

Site Purpose

The site provides information on the importance of happiness, debunks common myths about happiness, and offers steps to achieve happiness.

Scenarios

  • What are the key factors that contribute to happiness?
  • What are common myths about happiness that people believe?
  • How can I implement practical steps to become happier in my daily life?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • #2D2A32 (Raisin Black) - Used for headings and accents.
  • #DDD92A (Citrine) - Used for text and background elements.
  • #EAE151 (Maize) - Used for highlights and buttons.
  • #EEEFA8 (Vanilla) - Used for warnings and special notices.
  • #FAFDF6 (Baby Powder) - Used for background.
